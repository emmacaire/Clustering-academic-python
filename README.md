# Academic Project: Customer Clustering Analysis
**Institution:** NOVA IMS Lisboa | **Course:** Data Mining 1

## 📋 Summary
A clustering project involving customer sales data, including demographics and product detail as features to identify customer segments. The final clustering defined four different groups with specific characteristics in terms of income, life stage, sales channels and product preferences.
The performed segmentation helps identify which products and accessories are more relevant to the business, which segments should discounts be targeted at, and how to differentiate the pricing policy between online and in store sales, depending on the customer group's preferences.

## 📊 Key Deliverables & Artifacts
* 📂 **[Interactive Analysis Notebook](./notebooks/Clustering_data_mining_1.ipynb):** 
Python code including EDA, data cleaning, segmentation methods and final clustering.
* 📄 **[Full Academic Paper / PDF Report](./report/Clustering_report_DM1.pdf):** 
A synthetic report explaining the methodologies, challenges and summarizing the business implications on the market.
